About Us

PropKart is a modern real‑estate marketplace built to simplify how people discover, evaluate and buy property. We combine verified listings with rich details, high‑quality media and helpful tools so you can shortlist faster and decide with confidence.

Whether you’re looking for your first home, upgrading for space, or exploring investment opportunities, our platform surfaces the most relevant options based on your budget, location and lifestyle needs.

Transparency is core to our mission. We highlight key specs, provide clear pricing and show real engagement signals so you always know what to expect before you schedule a visit.

For sellers, PropKart offers an intuitive dashboard to publish listings, manage media, track views and enquiries, and keep buyers updated—all from one place.

We’re continuously improving discovery, communication and analytics so both buyers and sellers save time and move forward with clarity.
